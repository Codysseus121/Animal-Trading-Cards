Black panthers owe their colour to
a genetic mutation.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca, Panthera pardus
  • Group: Mammal
  • Location: Asia, Africa, America
  • Habitat: Forest, swampland and grasslands

The Panther is an incredibly intelligent and agile animal that is very seldom seen by people in the wild as they are generally very quiet and cautious animals. It is a solitary animal that leads a nocturnal lifestyle, spending much of the daylight hours resting safely high in the trees.
